Welcome to My Website!!!

A place where I share who I am, my experiences, and the things that shaped me.

About Me

My name is Tsheyang Tshogyal. I am from Mongar in the eastern side of Bhutan. I would call myself an introvert because I feel more comfortable in quiet places. I do not like big crowds and I enjoy spending time alone. Being alone helps me think, read, draw, or just relax. Over time, I have learned that being introverted is okay, and it even gives me time to think about things and explore my hobbies and interests.

My Childhood

When I was a child, I spent most of my time alone. I rarely played with other kids in my neighborhood. I liked drawing, painting, and doing activities by myself. I was born premature, so I had to go to Guwahati, India for health checkups often. Because of that, I spent a lot of time there and created many memories with my grandparents, especially my grandfather. Even though I am from Bhutan, part of my childhood was in Guwahati,India. I often went there for my health checkups and i basically grew up there. Guwahati is by childhood home and a special place for me filled with memories.

"I learned to enjoy my own company and found happiness in simple moments."

My Primary School Life

I studied in a private school with fewer students, which was exactly what I liked. The smaller environment made it easier to make friends and get help from teachers. I spent six years at Phuensum Primary School and created many memories there. I joined different activities and learned a lot, not just from lessons but from being part of a school community. My teachers and friends at Phuensum gave me support, encouragement, and love, which helped me grow and enjoy learning.

Hobbies & Interests

I enjoy calligraphy very much. My English teacher in primary school first taught me how to write in cursive, and she always encouraged me. That is how I started practicing calligraphy. It is fun and relaxing for me. When I do calligraphy, it reminds me of my school days and the happy memories I had. It is also a way to focus and improve patience, and I enjoy seeing the results of my work.

Family & Friends

My family has always been very supportive. They care for me and help me in many ways. They encourage me to follow my interests and support me when I face challenges. I also have great friends from primary school and high school who are very caring. They make life more fun and help me feel connected. My family and friends are very important to me and make me feel happy and safe.
